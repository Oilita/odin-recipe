Stone Age Bread Recipe

Only seeds, nuts and some eggs. What else do we need to make this mythical and delicious bread?

Dated from the nordic medieval times, this bread deserve its name Stone Age Bread.

You may wonder then "why the heck do I want something this old it must be difficult, right? No, my fellow baker!

Actually, it is so simple that you'll laugh while thinking why you never came with this bread before.

So, let's take a deep breath and dive in this nordic journey!

Here what will you need:

  • 100g walnuts
  • 100g almonds
  • 100g cashew nuts
  • 100g sunflower seeds
  • 100g pumpkin seeds
  • 100g sesame seeds
  • 50g flax seeds
  • 50g chia seeds
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup any good oil
  • 4 tsp brown sugar or honey
